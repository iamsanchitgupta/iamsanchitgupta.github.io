I am currently working as a Software Engineer in the Apps Search and Intelligence team at Google Bangalore. After graduating from IIIT Delhi in 2017, I have also interned with Microsoft Research under Dr. Venkat Padmanbhan on the HAMS Project. As a part of my undergrad, I worked with the Image Analysis and Biometrics lab under Dr. Richa Singh and Dr. Mayank Vatsa. My ultimate goal is to build a sustainable social enterprise to bridge the education gap in STEM, with a strong focus on underrepresented groups.

I have also been mentoring 400+ girls across 35+ colleges all over India. In my free time you can catch me playing Bullet Chess or Puzzle Rush. I formed Google Bangalore's Chess club under the guidance of Anand Rangarajan and The 65th Square, IIIT Delhi's Chess club under the guidance of Dr. Somitra Sanadhya. Other hobbies include: Writing, Stand-up Comedy, Cricket, Table Tennis, Movies and TV series.
